About

Luiz Gustavo is an 18-year-old front-end developer from Araras, São Paulo, Brazil. A violinist since the age of five, he uses his talent to serve God. Early on, he dreamed of being an architect or civil engineer, but his passion gradually shifted toward computers, games, and programming.

Creating has always driven him. What began with building model structures as a child evolved into crafting websites from scratch, blending creativity with functionality. He values European design and aims to deliver refined, immersive digital experiences, even within little technical limits.

Luiz is currently in his second semester of an Information Systems degree and is also learning French to broaden his personal and professional horizons.
